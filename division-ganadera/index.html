Division Ganadera

My Role

As a tech lead in a small startup, my role resides in driving the development of our product to a successful conclusion. I act as the bridge connecting the business side of the project with the technical, while making sure that both sides have the proper information to make good decisions that help us achieve our goals. I often have to wear many hats, some times planning, breaking down tasks, and managing timelines. Almost equally as often though I have to give a hand to the team when designing new features solving problems, mostly along the lines of a Fullstack dev.

The Team

In our 5-person development team, we combine our diverse skills to turn our startup's vision into reality.

Our engineers ensure a robust infrastructure, handling databases and server-side performance, while our talented frontend developers craft engaging user interfaces with modern web technologies. With clear communication and an agile approach, we work together seamlessly (or as seamlessly as possible) in order to make a positive impact with innovative solutions and a shared commitment to excellence.

The visionary behind Division Ganadera is our product owner, an agriculturural engineer with 20+ years of experience in the field. They act as the voice of our customers, with a deep understanding of their needs. They shape the product roadmap, prioritize features, and constantly validate our direction with market research and user feedback. Their role is essential in ensuring we stay on track and deliver value to our users.
